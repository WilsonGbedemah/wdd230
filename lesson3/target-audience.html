Audience

Personas

Businesses

Businesses are one of the main focuses of our website. We supply information useful to business owners, managers, analysts, etc. These individuals would likely be very familiar with our topic and well educated. These people are likely to use our site as a source of relevant, community and business information. We should be able to aid individuals indetermining how there business can be, or continue to be, successful. This resource will be especially beneficial to aspiring entrepreneurs looking to start a business, as it will provide information they might otherwise have trouble finding.

Investors

Our site also accomidates investors. These individuals will likely have at least a basic understanding of our topic, and fairly educated. As with businesses, we provide information useful to investors, which businesses are a safe venture, and which could bring the greatest reward. More generally, we provide information on various businesses and what opportunities are available with those businesses. We will also advertise investment and sponsorship opportunities, including events looking for sponsorship.

Scenarios

Entrepreneur

An entrepreneur is looking to start a business in our area. He needs information about the area in order to effectively start his business, or determine if it's viable in the first place. Our site provides him with information on other businesses in the area, and how they've fared. Additionally, we provide information on the community, and how the weather may affect their business.

Partial Owner

A partial owner of a company is looking to expand their business into our area. In order to convinse his partners/shareholders that the expansion is beneficial, he needs to find data to back up his proposal. Our site can provide him with data about the market in our area, including what has done well in the past, and what has failed.

Analyst

A company is looking to introduce a new product. They've assigned an analyst to to determine if the product would be well received by the community. He turns to our site, which informs him about other companies producing similar products in the area, and what that product is selling for.

Investor

An individual is looking to make several safe investments. Our site provides him with a list of businesses, and each businesses stock value, both current and in the past. With this information he can easily find a company that has consistently increased in value.

Sponsor

An individual is looking to advertise their business in our area. He finds several upcoming events advertised on our site as needing sponsors. From the site he can browse several events in our area, and find one that has an audience more likely to be interested in the service his business provides.
