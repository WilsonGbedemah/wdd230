Scenarios

Business Owner

a business owner seeks the services of any business in the field of insurance to help keep their business in safes hands. As a chamber of commerce we help provide the necessary information of all other business that offer such services of insurance to such a company with added information as to they rates and charges as well.

Investor

a local investor wanting to invest his money into a local business to provide more help to the community and also in the long run make profits, seeks the help from our website, and we do a work of categorizing all the business or companies available in the locality with information as to which business require or hand out partnerships or share to the public and also give tips as to what rates to expects from amount invested.

Shareholders

a particular set of shareholders have been in the locality for long want to know how to tap into their customers base in the locality for increased demand and also more profits as well and how the growth of the locality is progressing so they know how to proceed with their operations. With this in mind, the website provides these shareholders with potential customers whom can be termed as members and also information of the various development projects taken place in the locality and how it is affecting the entity they own or operate.

Customers

a customer in search of a particular product for purchase and with little or no idea as to where to find such a product can use out websites as it shows the list of operating businesses or companies with the various products being produced with the precise location where to find such product because it has marketing and advertising information of such businesses. With this website if such a product cannot be found it give information to other business that might provide such services in different locations as well.

Small Business

a small business owner who has some problems about the some practices of other companies which diminishes the status of similar business and seeking to raise such concerns about such issues can do with the chamber of commerce with the "contact us" section which is provided in the website and such issues may be discussed by the chamber whether at the musical level or taken to a higher level for more better and improved solutions for growth of all business and the community at large.
